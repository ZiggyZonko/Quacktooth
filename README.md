# QuackyTooth! 🦆🛜

QuackyTooth is my very own AUX to Bluetooth signal convertor, specifically designed for audio listening. It uses the MH-M18 bluetooth module, a few simple switches and a female AUX Output to easily convert the signals for effortless listening. It works by feeding the signal in through the AUX Port to the Bluetooth Module, controlling the music on command, which is powered by the 1000mAH 3.7V Battery secured in the case. 

- MH-M18 Bluetooth Module
- Capacitors
- AUX Female Port
- Battery
  
Is all you need to build this simple project!

This project is so beneficial to me, I decided to make it because I listen to music on the bus to school everyday, and most of the time, I would rather leave my phone at home so I don't get distracted in school. My watch is compatible with bluetooth, but I usually use wired headphones to avoid the EM waves bluetooth emits, especially when it's that close to your brain haha. So, that sparked my innovation, specifically for personal use. This will help me greatly not get distracted in school, while still enjoying my morning music.
